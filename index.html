📡 NFC Tags — How It Works

Stick NFC tags on your boxes and shelves. When you tap one with your phone, the app opens straight to the scan screen with that location already selected — no menus needed.

What to buy: NTAG213 stickers (~$10 for 20 on Amazon)
To write tags: NFC Tools app (free, iPhone & Android)
To read tags: Just tap — works automatically in Safari/Chrome

🔗 Your Location URLs

Enter your GitHub Pages URL to generate a scannable link for each location.

✍️ Writing Tags (NFC Tools app)

1. Open NFC Tools
2. Tap Write → Add a Record → URL/URI
3. Paste the URL for that location
4. Tap Write → hold phone over tag
5. Stick tag on your box or shelf ✓
